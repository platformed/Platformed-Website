About

About the team, and the game

Platformed is a free game where you can design and play user created levels. the games play mode allows players to play other poeple's levels, and test out their own, and can be used at anytime to test out a level simply by pressing the play button on the toolbar, making level design a fast and effeciant workflow.

The team is made up of 2 designers, and the game itself is being developed on the unity game engine, quality comes before quantity here at the platformed team, so we would like to hear what you think about our game over at our contact page
